# Changelog: v012 to v013
**Paper:** 005 - Autopoietic Cohomology: Iterative Obstruction Repair, Manifold Emergence, and Gauge Structure on Causal Posets
**Date:** March 2026

## Overview of Changes
This version completes the strict enforcement of claim discipline across the framework's mathematical structure. We have methodically downgraded absolute "forcing" mechanics to rigorously defined expectations and generic tendencies within bounding conditions, isolating the causal mechanisms strictly to the regions they have formally proven to govern.

## Specific Modifications

### 1. 2D Emergence Bounded
- **Target:** Remark 3.11 (Geometric Emergence)
- **Change:** Adjusted the absolute claim that "$ \ell^1 $ minimization forces 2-dimensional geometry" to specify that "within the autopoietic repair dynamics for scalar cochains, terminal complexes exhibit 2-dimensional combinatorial manifold structure".
- **Rationale:** Clarifies that the 2D requirement is a property emergent within the specific dynamics and constraints of the iterator on scalar cochains, rather than a universal dimensional fiat.

### 2. $N \ge 4$ Instability Downgraded
- **Target:** Lemma 3.8 and Theorem 3.9
- **Change:** Changed the absolute "No $N=4$ Configurations Persist" to "$N \ge 4$ Configurations are Generically Unstable," clarifying that the iterator is *expected* to resolve them under generic subgradient resolution, rather than structurally blocking their transient existence entirely.
- **Rationale:** Accurately reflects the bounds of the proof. We proved generic flat-valley instability triggering diagonal insertion, but tracking specific operator selection constraints requires a probabilistic/generic boundary rather than absolute deterministic annihilation.

### 3. Causal Bounds Sourced to Graph Topology
- **Target:** Corollary 6.7 (Emergent Causality)
- **Change:** Adjusted the final mechanism from explicitly being "forced" by optimization geometry to "emerging naturally in lattice-like regimes from $\ell^1$ optimization geometry."
- **Rationale:** The causality properties explicitly require the min-cut boundary conditions natively stable in lattice-like subgraphs; the new phrasing makes explicit its dependence on this topological class instead of universal structural dictation.

### 4. Convergence Uniqueness Relieved
- **Target:** Corollary 5.5 (Omega-Limit Fixed Point)
- **Change:** Modified to point out that distinct trajectory sequences converge to a fixed point, but "uniqueness of the limit independent of tie-breaking remains an open question," replacing the assertion that the omega-limit set intrinsically consists of exactly one definitive structure.
- **Rationale:** Prevents overclaiming uniqueness without a formal tie-breaking topological constraint, securing the proof of convergence limits.
